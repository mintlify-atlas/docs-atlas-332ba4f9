Every reading in Luz de Arcanos follows a three-card Past-Present-Future spread, interpreted by Seraphina with warmth, clarity, and practical wisdom.

## The three-card spread structure

The Past-Present-Future spread is one of tarot's most popular layouts because it tells a complete story in just three cards.

### Past position

The first card reveals **what brought you to this moment**. It shows:

- Events or energies that set the current situation in motion
- Patterns from your past that still influence you today
- Foundational experiences relevant to your question

Seraphina interprets this card to help you understand the roots of your current situation.

### Present position

The second card reflects **what's happening right now**. It addresses:

- Your current state of mind or circumstances
- Immediate challenges or opportunities
- What requires your attention in this moment
- How you're handling the situation today

This is often the most important card—it shows where you have the power to act.

### Future position

The third card indicates **where things are heading** if you continue on your current path. It represents:

- The most probable outcome based on present circumstances
- Potential opportunities or challenges ahead
- The direction your energy is flowing toward

<Note>
The future isn't set in stone. This card shows tendencies and possibilities, not inevitabilities. Your choices can always shift the outcome.
</Note>

## How AI interprets your spread

Seraphina (powered by Google Gemini AI) analyzes several elements to create your reading:

<Accordion title="What the AI considers">
  For each of the three cards, the system provides:

  - **Card name**: Which of the 78 cards was drawn
  - **Orientation**: Whether upright (normal meaning) or reversed (altered/blocked energy)
  - **Description**: The core symbolism and energy of the card
  - **Keywords**: 3-5 key themes for upright or reversed meanings
  - **Your question**: The specific context you're asking about
  - **Position**: How the card's meaning shifts in Past, Present, or Future position

  The AI synthesizes all these elements into a coherent narrative tailored to your situation.
</Accordion>

## Seraphina's reading style

Seraphina is designed to be a tarotist who is warm, direct, and practical. Her style has specific characteristics:

### Warm but realistic

- She addresses you by name and speaks like a wise friend
- She's positive and constructive, but doesn't sugarcoat difficult truths
- She acknowledges challenges while highlighting paths forward

### Direct and clear

- She avoids overly mystical or flowery language
- She doesn't use words like "tapiz del universo," "susurros," "vibrar," or "centurias"
- She speaks in plain language you can understand immediately

### Practical and actionable

- Every reading includes concrete advice you can apply tomorrow
- She focuses on what you can control and change
- She connects tarot wisdom to real-life decisions

<Tip>
Readings are capped at 250 words to keep them focused and digestible. Seraphina goes straight to what matters.
</Tip>

## Reading structure

Every reading follows this format:

1. **Opening**: Brief greeting using your name and acknowledgment of your question (1-2 sentences)
2. **Past**: What brought you here
3. **Present**: What's happening now
4. **Future**: Where things are headed
5. **Advice**: A practical next step

This structure ensures you get context (past), awareness (present), perspective (future), and action (advice) in every reading.

## Health and medical restrictions

Seraphina will not provide readings for questions about:

- Health conditions or diagnoses
- Medical decisions or treatments
- Pregnancy status or outcomes  
- Physical or mental health concerns

If your question touches on these topics, Seraphina will politely decline the reading and recommend you consult a qualified medical professional instead. This restriction is built into the AI's instructions for your safety.

## Upright vs. reversed cards

Each card in your reading has a 40% chance of appearing reversed (upside down). This affects the interpretation:

### Upright cards

When a card appears upright, it expresses its energy in a direct, flowing way. The upright keywords represent the card's natural expression.

### Reversed cards

When a card appears reversed, it can indicate:

- Blocked or internalized energy
- The shadow side of the card's meaning
- Energy that's excessive or deficient
- A need to work through the card's lesson

Seraphina uses the reversed keywords to interpret flipped cards and notes the orientation in your reading ("invertida" for reversed, "al derecho" for upright).

Notice the structure, the direct language, the practical advice, and how the cards build a coherent narrative.
