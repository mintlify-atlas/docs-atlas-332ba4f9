# Card Reference Overview

The Luz de Arcanos deck contains **78 tarot cards** divided into two main groups: the Major Arcana and the Minor Arcana. Each card carries unique symbolism, keywords, and interpretations that provide guidance and insight.

## The Tarot Structure

### Major Arcana (22 cards)

The Major Arcana represents life's significant spiritual lessons and karmic influences. These 22 cards (numbered 0-21) depict archetypal energies and major life themes:

- **0 - El Loco** through **21 - El Mundo**
- Represents the Fool's Journey from innocence to completion
- Signals important life events and spiritual milestones
- Carries more weight in readings than Minor Arcana cards

[Learn more about Major Arcana cards →](/cards/major-arcana/list)

### Minor Arcana (56 cards)

The Minor Arcana reflects day-to-day experiences and practical matters. These 56 cards are divided into four suits:

- **Bastos (Wands)** - Fire element, passion, creativity, action
- **Oros (Pentacles)** - Earth element, material world, prosperity
- **Copas (Cups)** - Water element, emotions, relationships, intuition
- **Espadas (Swords)** - Air element, thoughts, conflicts, clarity

Each suit contains 14 cards: Ace through 10, plus four court cards (Paje, Caballero, Reina, Rey).

## Quick Start Guide

1. **Familiarize yourself** with the Major Arcana first - these are the foundation of tarot wisdom
2. **Learn the suits** and their elemental associations
3. **Practice interpretations** by considering both upright and reversed meanings
4. **Trust your intuition** - keywords are guides, not rigid rules
