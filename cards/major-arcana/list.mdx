# Major Arcana Card List

The Major Arcana consists of 22 cards representing the most significant spiritual and life lessons. These cards depict the Fool's Journey - a metaphorical path from innocence (El Loco) to completion (El Mundo).

<Note>
  Major Arcana cards carry more weight in readings than Minor Arcana cards. They often indicate important life events, spiritual lessons, or karmic influences.
</Note>

## The Fool's Journey

The Major Arcana tells a story of spiritual evolution:

### Act I: The Material World (0-7)

From **El Loco's** innocent beginning through encounters with earthly authorities and lessons:
- Personal power (El Mago)
- Inner wisdom (La Sacerdotisa)
- Earthly abundance (La Emperatriz)
- Worldly authority (El Emperador)
- Spiritual teaching (El Hierofante)
- Heart's choices (Los Enamorados)
- Willpower triumphs (El Carro)

### Act II: Inner Journey (8-14)

Deeper spiritual lessons and internal transformation:
- Inner strength (La Fuerza)
- Solitary wisdom (El Ermitaño)
- Karmic turning points (La Rueda de la Fortuna)
- Universal justice (La Justicia)
- Willing sacrifice (El Colgado)
- Necessary endings (La Muerte)
- Balanced integration (La Templanza)

### Act III: Spiritual Awakening (15-21)

Confronting shadow and achieving enlightenment:
- Shadow work (El Diablo)
- Tower moments (La Torre)
- Renewed hope (La Estrella)
- Mysterious depths (La Luna)
- Illumination (El Sol)
- Higher calling (El Juicio)
- World integration (El Mundo)

<Tip>
  When multiple Major Arcana cards appear in a reading, pay special attention. This suggests the situation carries significant spiritual weight or represents a major life transition.
</Tip>
